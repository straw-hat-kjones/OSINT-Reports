# IntelBroker

## Overview

**IntelBroker** (real name: **Kai Logan West**) is a British cybercriminal who operated as one of the most prolific data breach actors from 2023-2025. He served as owner and administrator of BreachForums from August 2024 to January 2025 and was arrested in France in February 2025.
